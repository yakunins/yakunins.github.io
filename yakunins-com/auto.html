Redesign of m.auto.ru

In a first step I inspect the old website and analyze users’ behavior with Yandex.Metrica (Google Analytics analogue). I noticed major problems in short presentation. After revision of first scetches, I made mockups of homepage, advanced search page and some inner pages.


Office — the website for working with car dealers

The website made up of two main parts, client’s part and administration part. We created interface map of the site.

Client’s part: here car dealer can manage its ads, contact and other information, setup automatic upload of bulk of ads and photos.

Administration part: here administrator can add, remove or block car dealer and its users. Whithin this interface administrator also can moderate user’s edits.

Pages of technical support allow to track automatic upload of bulks of ads. Newsletter subscription template helps to tell about new possibilities.

The work on the interface was carried out in several stages, from January to April 2014. Over the time, the description of website grows into 40 pages in Confluence.

Banner ad campaigns scheduler

This interface answers the question ‘how many ad slots are used and not used’. It shows banner rotation stats, visualize scheduled campaigns, gives an ability to asses advertising opportunities in many different parts of the website.

Billing popups wizard

The task was to give an ability to order the service and to explain terms of service.
